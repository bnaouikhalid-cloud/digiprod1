Answering the hard questions

What business owners want clarified before they change anything.

Will my team actually use this?
Flexcel is designed around Excel and WhatsApp—the tools many teams already use. DigiProd maps automation behind those habits so employees are not asked to maintain another disconnected system.
Do we have to replace Excel or Tally?
No replacement is implied. The current DigiProd positioning is to connect existing sheets, WhatsApp inputs and Tally records where the agreed workflow requires them.
Can we start with one process?
Yes. Flexcel is described as modular, with a recommendation to begin at the narrowest bottleneck and expand once the team gains confidence.
Who owns the data and custom application IP?
DigiProd’s current site states that the client owns its business data and that custom-application intellectual property belongs to the client organization. Contract language should confirm this for each engagement.
What happens when our process changes?
The implementation approach includes calibration around real edge cases and planned changes. Scope, support and commercial terms should be agreed for each module before launch.
