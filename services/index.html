Structural clarity

Common questions before choosing a technology partner.

Can you work with the software we already have?
Yes. DigiProd’s existing service position is to build modern bridges around tools such as Excel and Tally where practical. The audit determines what can be connected safely and what should remain separate.
How do we choose between Fractional CTO and a Shared Team?
Choose Fractional CTO when the primary need is direction, risk review and governance. Choose a Shared Team when the roadmap is clearer but execution capacity is missing. Some engagements may combine both.
How fast can we launch?
The current site describes quick wins followed by wider delivery. A credible launch plan depends on scope, access, existing-system quality, approvals and test coverage, so timing is confirmed after the audit.
Will staff need technical training?
The goal is to preserve recognizable habits and keep technology behind the work. Any new portal or field tool is still designed and tested around the people who will use it.
Who owns our code and data?
DigiProd’s current site says clients retain their business data and custom-application IP. The signed statement of work should specify repositories, accounts, credentials, exports and handover responsibilities.
Can you maintain software another team built?
Potentially. Product-support work begins with a technical, security and ownership audit so DigiProd can identify code quality, access and continuity risks before committing to a support model.
