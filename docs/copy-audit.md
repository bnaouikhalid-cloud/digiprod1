## Source copy deliberately not carried into launch copy

These items are documented but excluded until the client supplies evidence and approval:

- 97% / 83% on-time delivery claims.
- 98% adoption, 100% process coverage, 92% mistakes avoided, 85% retention, 4× response speed and 30% savings.
- Exact use-case performance percentages.
- 8.9/10, 95%, 72%, 50+, two-hour and similar aggregate proof claims.
- “Bank-grade,” “military-grade,” “zero vulnerability,” “guaranteed growth” and other security/guarantee superlatives.
- Universal one-week, 7–10 day, 10-day and 6–10 week promises.
- Pricing and cost-comparison figures, which require current commercial validation.
- “No licence fees,” which conflicts with the visible Office and maintenance requirements.
- All testimonial impact labels, even where exact qualitative quotes are retained.
- 120+ vs 150+ founder/customer totals.
- External publication-logo recognition claims.
