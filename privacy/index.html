Prototype notice

How information is handled in this redesign

The enquiry forms in this static project are non-transmitting demonstrations. Validation and success states happen only in the browser so the interaction can be reviewed safely.

Information a production form may request

Name, work contact details, company, business size, the type of help requested and an optional description of the workflow or technology problem.

Why that information may be used

To respond to the enquiry, understand the requested service, arrange a relevant conversation and maintain a record of business communication where permitted.

Production responsibilities

Before connecting a live endpoint, DigiProd should approve the final privacy notice, lawful basis, retention period, access controls, service providers, deletion process and any required cookie or analytics disclosures.
